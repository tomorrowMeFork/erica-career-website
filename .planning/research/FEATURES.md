# Feature Research

**Created:** 2026-05-03  
**Domain:** University career services, job aggregation, and AI career assistants.

## Comparable Product Signals

University AI career systems such as Waterloo JADA, Lancaster LUCA, Ohio Theo, University of Miami's Toppel AI Agent, and Korean university tools including Korea University HORANG2, Wonkwang CarHub, Jeonbuk AI JOB, and DeepResume-style systems converge on several patterns:

- Students need a single place to search jobs, ask process questions, and understand next actions.
- AI career tools work best when tied to institution-specific data instead of generic career advice.
- Job recommendations need explicit match reasons to earn trust.
- Career centers position AI as a complement to human advising, not a replacement.
- Privacy, student-data control, and responsible-AI statements are part of the product, not legal afterthoughts.

## Table Stakes

| Category | Features |
|---|---|
| Source-grounded Q&A | Korean natural-language questions, answers grounded in ERICA/Hanyang source data, citations, no-answer/refusal behavior |
| Job discovery | Aggregated public listings, source links, deadlines, freshness, source/category filters |
| Recommendations | Preference-based matching by major, job type, industry, region, employment type, deadline sensitivity |
| Explainability | Match reasons, source cards, confidence/uncertainty labels, visible official-page verification path |
| User control | Preference editing, clearing controls, non-personalized fallback mode |
| Career-service fit | Disclaimers, human advisor complement positioning, support for official source verification |
| Evaluation | Citation accuracy checks, no-answer cases, Korean answer quality checks, retrieval recall tests |

## Differentiators for ERICA Career Chat

- Employment success-story intelligence: answer questions using Hanyang success stories with exact citations.
- ERICA-specific source understanding: focus on CDP and campus/college-level boards before generic public job boards.
- Korean-first conversational UX with source cards and deadline-aware listings.
- Explicit preference matching that explains why a listing is relevant.
- Future curriculum/program linkage after the source-grounded MVP proves useful.

## Deferred v2 Features

- Resume upload and job-fit analysis.
- Cover-letter or 자기소개서 drafting assistance.
- Interview preparation from selected postings.
- Saved-job/application tracker.
- Alumni or senior mentoring/coffee-chat matching.
- Proactive reminders through email/KakaoTalk or official university channels.
- Official SSO and staff admin tools, if authorization becomes available.

## Anti-Features

- General ChatGPT clone behavior not grounded in ERICA source data.
- Automated job applications or outreach on behalf of students.
- Engagement-maximizing infinite chat loops.
- Ads, recruiting marketplace behavior, or opaque employer promotion.
- Career tests that lock students into rigid paths.
- Fully generated resumes or claims without student-provided evidence.
