<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| User/source seed docs → registry | Seed URLs and approval claims become machine-readable policy that downstream code may enforce. |
| Human checklist → future ingestion | Human review outcomes may later permit fetch methods and must be explicit, auditable, and limited. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-01-05 | Spoofing | Approval basis text | mitigate | Use `approval_basis: user_assertion`; checklist explicitly says it is not official Hanyang authorization. |
| T-01-06 | Elevation of Privilege | Seed scope | mitigate | Registry contains exactly six seed intents from D-14; no unrelated Hanyang domains or ERICA pages. |
| T-01-07 | Denial of Service | Future collection posture | mitigate | Store `moderate_1_2s_low_concurrency`, backoff/stop language, and `scheduled_crawling_enabled: false` for every record. |
| T-01-08 | Information Disclosure | Future credentials | mitigate | Checklist documents `.env`-only credentials and no committed/logged secrets; no secret values are created in this plan. |
</threat_model>
